How rcrd works

Cats

Cats, short for categories, are things I want to track. Cats are separated by commas (white space doesn't matter). Many cats make up one record.

For example, I record every time I get a haircut. After I walk out of the barber shop, I'll type something like this:

haircut, Joe's Barber Shop

Which is displayed like this:

<%= render partial: "shared/cat", locals: {cat: "haircut", hue: hue_now} %> <%= render partial: "shared/cat", locals: {cat: "Joe's Barber Shop", hue: hue_now} %>

Magnitudes

Numbers before a cat will not be interpreted as the cat's name. Instead, they will be stored along with the cat.

workout, run, 5.3 miles, Presidio

<%= render partial: "shared/cat", locals: {cat: "workout", hue: hue_now} %> <%= render partial: "shared/cat", locals: {cat: "run", hue: hue_now} %> <%= render partial: "shared/cat", locals: {cat: "5.3 miles", hue: hue_now} %> <%= render partial: "shared/cat", locals: {cat: "Presidio", hue: hue_now} %>

Clicking on '5.3 miles' will show you records with 'mile' cats.
